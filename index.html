Interest in IT

I’ve had an interest in IT since I was born, from the latest game console on the market, to the latest
advancement in the technology in phones. The smallest feature added to new technology is always the
thing that excites me the most. When you look at pictures of the first iPhone that was created you can
see how small (yet chunky) and useless a mobile phone was, with little to no features, now I look in my
pocket and see a computer that can do everything that the very first iPhone could do and 1000 other
things. This advancement had taken place in under 10 years, with new phones coming out every year, each
phone better and more efficient than the last. The amount of hardware that was engineered to fit into
a such a small device amazes me, compared to 10 years ago and inspires me to achieve the same level of
advancement in the coming years. I want to be able to help pioneer technology into the next generation
of technology as phone companies have done over the years. Not only phone companies like apple and
Samsung, but other companies that have created future tech in the present era like Tesla and google.
Tesla is another major company that inspires me to want to create technology that no one has seen before
and technology that is believed to be "of the future". As many others, Elon Musk is an idol of mine.
His journey from small town kid, to creating PayPal, to creating SpaceX and now Tesla, inspires me to
continue studying in the IT industry and hopefully get the chance to work on projects within his companies.
